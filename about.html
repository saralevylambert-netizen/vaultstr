Our story

We built the resource we wished existed.

When we were scaling our own STR portfolio, the research process was broken. Review sites were full of affiliate-driven rankings where the highest bidder sat at the top. Vendor websites described every feature in superlatives. Operator forums had great instincts but no consistent evaluation criteria.

So we built VaultSTR — a single place where every tool in the STR operator's stack is evaluated using the same rubric, tested by operators with real portfolios, and ranked on performance, not ad spend.
